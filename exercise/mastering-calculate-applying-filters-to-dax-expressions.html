The CALCULATE function is one of the most powerful and frequently used functions in DAX. It evaluates an expression in a modified filter context. This means you can specify new filters that will be applied during the calculation, or you can remove or override existing filters. CALCULATE is crucial for creating measures that perform calculations under different conditions than the current report context, such as 'Sales for Category A' regardless of the selected category.

The `CALCULATE` function is the most powerful and fundamental function in DAX, allowing you to modify the filter context for an expression. You'd use it to create custom measures that answer specific business questions, such as calculating Total Sales for Online Channel *regardless* of any customer or product filters applied to your report. This enables crucial comparisons, like sales percentage of total, or target vs. actuals, by forcing or removing filters within a measure's calculation.

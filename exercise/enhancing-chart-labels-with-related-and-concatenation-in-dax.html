The `RELATED` function is incredibly useful when you need to pull a single value, like a 'Product Category' or 'Customer Name', from a related dimension table (the 'one' side of your relationship) into a calculated column on your fact table (the 'many' side). This allows you to enrich your transaction-level data with descriptive attributes from dimension tables, simplifying reporting and subsequent calculations without requiring complex lookups.
